## 🔬 Key Research Areas

### 1. Mitochondrial Genetics – Oncogenesis & Chemotherapy Resistance

Mitochondria are not just the cell's powerhouses; they are key regulators of **ROS** production and **apoptosis** pathways. My research focuses on how **mtDNA mutations** alter cancer cell metabolism and induce drug resistance.

In a comprehensive 2025 study, **"Comprehensive Analysis of the Role of Mitochondrial DNA Mutations in MT-ND1, MT-ND5, and MT-CYB Genes,"** we demonstrated that mutations in `MT-ND1` and `MT-CYB` are significantly associated with tumor metastasis and reduced chemotherapy efficacy (`p < 0.05`). These mutations disrupt electron flow, increase ROS production, stabilize `HIF-1α`, and shift metabolism toward aerobic glycolysis (**Warburg effect**), promoting cancer cell survival.

Additionally, analysis of `A1555G`, `A3243G`, and `A7445G` mutations in cervical cancer patients revealed that the `A3243G` mutation in the `tRNALeu(UUR)` gene strongly correlates with chemotherapy resistance. This mutation reduces mitochondrial protein translation efficiency, raising the threshold for apoptosis induction.

---

### 2. Auditory Genetics – Mechanisms of Nonsyndromic Deafness

My research on nonsyndromic deafness highlights the critical role of mitochondrial DNA in sensory disorders. Investigation of `A1555G` and `A7445G` mutations showed that the `A1555G` mutation in the `12S rRNA` gene dramatically increases susceptibility to aminoglycoside antibiotic-induced ototoxicity. This mutation makes human `12S rRNA` structurally resemble bacterial ribosomes, leading to antibiotic binding, protein synthesis arrest, and permanent hearing loss.

---

### 3. Phytotherapy – Selective Cytotoxicity & Targeted Cell Death

A major challenge in oncology is the severe side effects of conventional chemotherapy. My research focuses on identifying natural compounds with **selective cytotoxicity**.

In an upcoming 2026 study, we evaluated the effects of **Polygala tenuifolia** extract on breast cancer cells (`MCF-7`) and normal epithelial cells (`MCF-10A`). The extract selectively reduced `MCF-7` cell viability by **35.2%** at 100 μg/mL, with no effect on normal cells. Mechanistically, it inhibited `GPX4` and `mTOR` pathways while upregulating `p53` and `TGF-β` expression, leading to a **2.41-fold increase** in lipid peroxidation and ferroptotic cell death.

Comparative studies (2021) on hydroalcoholic extracts of Pennyroyal, Summer Savory, Peppermint, and Tarragon confirmed their cytotoxic effects on breast cancer cell lines (`MCF-7`, `T-47D`, `MDA-MB-231`) through oxidative stress and caspase-dependent apoptosis.

---

### 4. Regenerative Medicine – Nanobiotechnology & Acute Wound Healing

My research in tissue regeneration combines **phytotherapy** with **nanotechnology** for enhanced wound healing.

In a study published in the **Journal of Skin and Stem Cell (2017)**, we evaluated a combination of sesame oil and pumpkin extract on third-degree burns in BALB/c mice. Histopathological analysis (**Mallory-Trichrome staining**) demonstrated significant increases in dermis thickness, tissue integrity restoration, and hair follicle regrowth (`P < 0.05`), with less scarring compared to standard treatments.

Furthermore, a study on **biosynthetic Nano-Titanium Dioxide** combined with sesame seed extract for second-degree burns showed that the photocatalytic properties of nanoparticles control infection, while sesame oil accelerates epithelialization. I have also contributed to the sol-gel auto-combustion synthesis of **Samarium-doped copper ferrite nanoparticles** (`CuFe2-xSmxO4`) for biophotocatalytic applications.

---

### 5. Sports Immunology – Cytokine Regulation in Autoimmune Diseases

A 2026 study investigated the effects of 12 weeks of Pilates exercises on serum levels of **IL-1β** (pro-inflammatory) and **IL-10** (anti-inflammatory) in **Multiple Sclerosis (MS)** patients. The findings demonstrate how mechanical stress from targeted exercises balances `Th1/Th17` and `Treg` immune cells, suppressing neuroinflammation in the central nervous system.
